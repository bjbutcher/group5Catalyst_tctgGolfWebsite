About Us

Burning Ridge Golf Club is a course with a fun layout and a character of its own. Whether you’re a high handicap golfer looking for an enjoyable round, or a scratch golfer looking for a great test of your game, Burning Ridge can accommodate your need. It is said that if you can play well here, you can play well anywhere! We hope you’ll come see this for yourself.

Burning Ridge boasts a large membership, and we are constantly planning fun events for them. We also run more tournaments than most in the Grand Strand area.

We are known as a very woman-friendly club, and this is reflected in our large contingent of female members and ladies groups. We have two women’s leagues: the Ridge Runners, a member group of 16-20 who play once a week, and the Palmetto Ladies League, 24-40 women who consider Burning Ridge their home course. Our women’s tees are thoughtfully laid out for meaningful play, and our women-member-designed lounge, with its great attention to detail, attests to our women-friendly philosophy.

The Burning Ridge history originally began as a 36-hole course, divided into an East course and a West course. The West course was closed for residential development in 2005, at which time the East course underwent extensive renovation.

Located in central Myrtle Beach on Highway 501, the primary artery into Myrtle Beach, Burning Ridge Golf Club is only 10 miles to the Atlantic Ocean and the heart of Myrtle Beach. Consider playing a round at Burning Ridge on your way into Myrtle Beach or on your way back home.
